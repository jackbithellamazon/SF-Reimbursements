MSC Logic

How MSC re-submissions are suggested.

When an MSC is denied, the next submission pre-fills at this percentage below the previous attempt. Set to 0 to always re-submit at the same value.

Case Follow-Up & Escalation

Once a case is open, the Action Queue chases Amazon for you on this schedule.

A case with no reply stays in "Monitor only" until Amazon's expected response window passes, then it moves into "Do Today" as overdue to respond. After the follow-up threshold it's marked follow up, and after the escalation threshold it's flagged escalate (red).

Cost Logic

COG comes from the SKU only, gross (VAT-inclusive). No prep fee, no adjustments — just COG.

SKU only, gross (inc VAT) — example: Amuk_7.65_0802_0_Jac_12 = £7.65
Net = (Reimbursement + linked adjustments) − (COG × Quantity)
COG is parsed from SKU first and treated as gross/VAT-inclusive. If a SKU has no readable COG, the app tries an exact ASIN/FNSKU match from known rows before blocking it. SellerFuse cost is ignored for decisions. Adjustment credits are linked to likely original rows before judging shortfall.

Your data lives in this browser only. Download a backup regularly — if the browser's data is ever cleared, anything not backed up can't be recovered.
