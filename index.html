Once a case is open, the Action Queue chases Amazon for you on this schedule.

A case with no reply stays in "Monitor only" until Amazon's expected response window passes, then it moves into "Do Today" as overdue to respond. After the follow-up threshold it's marked follow up, and after the escalation threshold it's flagged escalate (red).

Cost Logic

COG comes from the SKU only, gross (VAT-inclusive). No prep fee, no adjustments — just COG.

SKU only, gross (inc VAT) — example: Amuk_7.65_0802_0_Jac_12 = £7.65
Net = Reimbursement − (COG × Quantity)
COG is parsed from the SKU only and is treated as gross/VAT-inclusive. SellerFuse's own cost and net columns are ignored. If reimbursement is below COG it's a loss to work; at or above COG, no action. If the SKU has no readable COG, the row is blocked in Import Errors for the VA to enter it.
